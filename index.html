Who We Are???

We are Kamakshi & Harrshit—a husband-and-wife team with a shared passion for storytelling and celebrating life’s most meaningful moments. Together, we run IClickStudiOZ, a boutique destination wedding photography and filmmaking firm based in the vibrant city of Jodhpur, India. At IClickStudiOZ, we believe that your wedding is not just an event—it’s the beginning of a legacy. Our mission is to preserve the beauty, emotions, and unforgettable moments of your big day through heartfelt and timeless photography and films. We feel truly honored to be a part of such significant milestones and are grateful to the couples who trust us with their stories. Our Style and Approach For us, the magic of weddings lies in the authenticity of real, unscripted moments. From joyful tears to spontaneous laughter, we specialize in capturing the emotions that make every wedding unique. Our style is a blend of photojournalism, artistic portraiture, and documentary filmmaking, all infused with our creative vision and attention to detail. Our approach is subtle and unobtrusive, allowing the day to unfold naturally while we capture its essence with honesty and care. With Kamakshi by the bride’s side and Harrshit with the groom, we ensure every parallel narrative is beautifully documented, creating a complete and cohesive story. At IClickStudiOZ, we don’t just create pictures or films—we craft heirlooms that reflect who you are and preserve the magic of your story for generations. When your family looks back on these memories, we want them to feel the joy, love, and excitement of your wedding as if they were right there with you.
